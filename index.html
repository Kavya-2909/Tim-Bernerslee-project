I am the founder of WWW: World Wide Web

Timothy John Berners-Lee is, together with his manager at the time, the Belgian Robert Cailliau, the inventor and founder of the world wide web or worldwide web. He worked on this when he was a consultant software engineer at CERN in Switzerland from June to December 1990.
